How Weekly Session Generation Works

Use this page to auto-generate study sessions from your existing goals and availability template.

Before you generate

  • Create your goals inside your subject(s).
  • Set up your availability plan template so the generator can place sessions in valid time slots.

What happens next

  • The generator creates a weekly plan with session and sub-session slots based on your availability.
  • You review the generated sub-sessions and keep only the ones that work for you.
  • When you save, selected items become normal sessions and appear in your Sessions list page.
